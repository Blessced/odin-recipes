lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked until golden and bubbly. Every bite combines savory flavors, a hearty texture, and comforting warmth, making it a timeless favorite for family meals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 11/2 teaspoon salt
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounces) package lasagna noodles
