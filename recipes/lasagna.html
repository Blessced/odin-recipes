lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked until golden and bubbly. Every bite combines savory flavors, a hearty texture, and comforting warmth, making it a timeless favorite for family meals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 11/2 teaspoon salt
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounces) package lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is brownned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and stir until well combined and heated through
  4. Stir mozarella, cottage cheese, and Parmesan together in a large bowl
  5. Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker
  6. Add a double layer of uncooked lasagna noodles, breaking noodles to fit onto cooked as needed
  7. Top noodles with a portion of cheese mixture
  8. Repeat the layering of sauce, noodles, and cheese until all the ingredients are used.
  9. Cover and cook on low until the lasagna noodles are tender, about 4 to 6 hours.
